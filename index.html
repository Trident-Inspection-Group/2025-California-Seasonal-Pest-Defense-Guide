Home Defense Blueprint

Structural Protection Checklist

  • Install gutter extensions that channel water 6 feet from foundation
  • Inspect leaks using an infrared thermometer every 2 weeks
  • Apply silica desiccant in crawl spaces
  • Seal all entry gaps with copper mesh and expandable foam

Safer, Smarter Building Materials

  • Replace wood mulch with 18" of river rock around foundation
  • Treat wood with 0.5% borate solution for long-term protection
  • Install stainless steel mesh on vents to prevent pest entry
  • Regularly inspect attics and crawl spaces for moisture issues

Termite Red Flags

  • Tap and listen for clicking or "head-banging" noises in walls and woodwork
  • Look for frass: drywood = pellets, subterranean = mud tunnels or tubes
  • Watch for swarming between 10AM‚Äì2PM on humid days, especially after rain
